Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our services, pricing, and support policies below.

1 What services does Davin Media offer?

We are a full-spectrum solutions partner specializing in four key areas: Web Development (design, build, and maintenance), Digital Marketing (SEO, PPC, Social), Training (upskilling teams in digital proficiency), and Job/Staff Recruiting (finding talent for digital roles).

2 Why should we choose Davin Media over a specialized agency?

Our multi-service approach ensures cohesion and efficiency. By having one partner handle strategy, execution, training, and talent acquisition, we guarantee that your website, marketing efforts, internal skills, and staff all work together toward a unified business goal.

3 Do you work with businesses outside of Abuja? >

Yes, we work with clients nationally and globally. While we enjoy local partnerships, the nature of our digital services allows us to collaborate effectively with remote teams through virtual meetings and advanced project management tools.

1 How do we get a specific price quote?

The best way is to schedule a free Discovery Call with our team. We'll discuss your goals, assess your current needs, and then provide a detailed, no-obligation proposal with a transparent cost breakdown.

2 What is the typical payment structure for a project (e.g., a new website)?

For project-based work, we generally require a 50% deposit to begin work, followed by milestone payments aligned with project completion stages. Ongoing marketing and training retainers are typically billed monthly in advance.

3 Are there any long-term contracts for your marketing services?

While we recommend a minimum 6-month commitment for Digital Marketing (as results take time), we offer flexible contract structures. After an initial commitment period, services often transition to a rolling month-to-month agreement.

1 How do I request support for my website after it launches?

We offer various post-launch support packages, from emergency fixes to proactive monthly maintenance (security updates, backups, performance checks). You can submit support requests directly to your Account Manager or via our client ticketing portal.

2 How often will we receive reports on our marketing performance?

We provide detailed, action-oriented performance reports monthly (or bi-weekly for high-intensity campaigns). These reports cover key metrics (e.g., traffic, conversion rate, ROAS) and our strategic plan for the next period.

3 Do you provide a guarantee for placed recruits?

Yes. We offer a standard [e.g., 90-day] replacement guarantee for all full-time placements. If the recruit leaves or is dismissed for cause within that period, we will find a suitable replacement at no additional placement fee.
